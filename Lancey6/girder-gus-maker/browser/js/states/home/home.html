Who is Gus?

Gus is your down to earth construction worker in a world where nothing is down to earth. While working on a construction site, Gus was transported to the Chaos Dimension where ordinary physics fly out the window. Even worse, Gus's tools were all lost during the trip and scattered throughout!

Fortunately for Gus, the Chaos Dimension is a lot like just another construction site. Thanks to his years of training on dangerous sites, and quirks in the gravity of the Chaos Dimension, Gus is a master of navigating the perilous unknown. Can Gus find all his tools and return home safely, or will be be trapped forever?
